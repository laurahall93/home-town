Most popular things to do in Riga

Visit the Old Riga

It is the city’s very heart, showcasing well-preserved medieval architecture and narrow cobbled streets at every corner.

Walking tour of Art Nouveau Architecture

Riga is an Art Nouveau wonderland, with more than 800 buildings, a third of the city’s stock. This is the world’s largest collection of Art Nouveau architecture,

Explore Central Market

Included in Riga’s UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Riga Central Market is one of the largest and most visited markets in Eastern Europe.
